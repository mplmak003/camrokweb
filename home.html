About Us

We are an engineering and construction company providing quality service to our clients with the vision of becoming a key player in engineering and construction across South Africa.

Overview

Camrok Construction was established in 2010 and has since been providing quality construction, engineering and management services.

Mission

We are a company with a mission to play a significant role in the world of engineering and construction, by establishing ourselves both locally and internationally, being motivated by the need to ensure valuable service delivery for our clients that strives to maintain and exceed service excellence while paying attention to safety, cost, and timely delivery.

Objectives

We have adopted ourselves to a mission statement “service excellence” that is used to measure our performance and mission, inter alia:

  • Execute all technical challenges to provide excellent solutions that will exceed the client’s expectations.
  • Employment equity will be maintained across all races and gender while aligning with Government’s stance towards empowerment.
  • Empower our staff to becoming a strong technical team and be recognized by the engineering and professional institutions.
  • Providing in-service training to graduates and part-time students.
